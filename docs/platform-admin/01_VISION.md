# Wegn Platform Admin — Vision

## Platform Name

**Wegn Platform Admin**

The internal operating system used by the Wegn team to deploy, monitor, support, and grow every business using any Wegn product.

## Purpose

Wegn Platform Admin is the central operating system for the Wegn ecosystem. It enables the Wegn team to onboard customers, monitor business health, provide support, manage subscriptions, oversee deployments, and operate every Wegn product from one unified platform.

## Core Principles (LOCKED)

1. **Global by Design**
Support multiple countries without redesign. Payment methods, currencies, taxes, languages, regulations, and business practices must be configurable by country.

2. **One Platform**
A single platform manages every Wegn product including Wegn-Store, QR-Wegn, QRBooker, Wegn Events, and future products.

3. **Customer First**
The platform exists to help customers succeed, not simply to administer software.

4. **Simple by Default**
Favor simple, intuitive workflows. Avoid unnecessary complexity. Build only what provides clear value.

5. **Scalable**
Design for growth from a handful of customers to thousands without requiring architectural redesign.

6. **Secure**
Protect customer data through strong authentication, tenant isolation, and secure operational practices.

7. **Reliable**
The platform should remain dependable and resilient, including in regions with unreliable internet connectivity.

8. **Partner Ecosystem**
Wegn Platform Admin is designed to support a global network of trusted partners and distributors.

Approved partners may onboard customers, deploy products, provide training, deliver support, and manage customer relationships within their assigned territories.

Wegn maintains centralized visibility, governance, security, and oversight across the entire ecosystem.

9. **Business-Centric**
A business is the primary customer of the Wegn ecosystem.

A business may subscribe to one or many Wegn products while maintaining a single identity, relationship, and history across the platform.

10. **Offline-Resilient**
Wegn products should continue supporting essential business operations during temporary internet disruptions whenever practical.

When connectivity returns, the platform should safely synchronize data without compromising integrity.

## Vision Statement

The platform is designed for internal Wegn teams first, while supporting trusted partners and approved automation as the ecosystem grows.
